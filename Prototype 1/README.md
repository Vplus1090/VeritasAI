## ğŸ” Overview

**VeritasAI** is a forensic intelligence platform designed to audit documents, detect fraud, and provide legal risk assessments in real-time.

Instead of a single AI model, VeritasAI employs a **Multi-Agent System** where specialized AI agents (The Accountant, The Bloodhound, The Skeptic) analyze documents independently before a "Chief Justice" agent synthesizes a final verdict. This architecture mimics a real-world forensic team, reducing hallucinations and increasing audit accuracy.
